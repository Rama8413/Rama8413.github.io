MAP SYSTEM — SHADOW & CCTV

SHADOW (Dark Spot)

· Always invisible while inside shadow
· Visible if hit by light, or in same shadow as another player
· Shadow locations: alleys, behind buildings, parking areas, hospital hallways, etc.

LIGHT

· Cooldown: 6s | Duration: 1s
· Effect: Reveals players in shadow
· Night time: Cooldown 3s, Duration 2s

CCTV

· Function: Lights dark spots, detects enemies, applies Mark
· Mark: Touch enemy (1 target only)
· Mark Duration: 12s
· Mark Effect: Exposed enemy location + red outline
· Disable: Approach and interact for 6s → CCTV disabled for 48s

NIGHT TIME

· More shadows
· Street lights flicker randomly (on 1-10s, off 1-5s)
· Light off = Shadow | Light on = No shadow
MAP FEATURES CAN AFFECT GAMEPLAY:
· Vents, air ducts, water pipes, trampolines, water, shadow.
