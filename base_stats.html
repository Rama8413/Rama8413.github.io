BASE SYSTEM

Inventory Rule

Cannot get 2 of the same item from inventory.

Stats

Base Max HP = 100
Base Max Mana = 100
Max cap HP and Mana = 250 / 250
When dead = Reset to 100 HP and 100 Mana.

Base Global Stats

Base Damage = 10 per second
Base Regen = 0.5 per second
Base Speed = 16
Base Cooldown = 1 second

Dash and Jump

Mana cost = 5
You can cancel stun with jump and dash.
Can dodge or avoid attack if really accurate.
Can lunge attack with dash. It is all about timing.
If you dash and hit a wall when dashing, you get flat and stun for 1 second.

Regen

Regen 1 Mana every 1 second.
If you take no damage for 10 seconds = Regen 5 HP.

Status Effects

Stun, Increase Speed, Reduce Speed, Increase Damage, Reduce Damage
Increase Damage Taken, Reduce Damage Taken, Increase Jump, Reduce Jump
Ragdoll Stun, Freeze, Regen HP, Reduce HP, Regen Mana, Reduce Mana
Blind Screen, Invisibility, Glitch, Frostbite, Poison, Burn
Lifesteal, Force Shield, Knockback, Pull, Knock up
Gain Stack, Reduce Stack, Cooldown Increase, Cooldown Reduction

Map System

Shadow = invisible inside dark spots. Visible if light hits you or same shadow as another player.
Light = cooldown 6s, duration 1s. Night time = 3s cooldown, 2s duration.
CCTV = light dark spot, detect enemy, marking. Mark lasts 12s.
Disable CCTV = approach and interact 6s, dies for 48s.
Night time = more shadows, street lamps blink random. Back To Main Page
